1 Speech input

Record your voice or upload speech files. Multiple files will be pooled for spectral analysis and noise generation.

Drop WAV / MP3 / MP4 files here, or click to browse

2 Generate speech-weighted noise

Superimpose randomised copies of the speech to create noise with the same spectral shape.

Default 10,000. For long recordings, fewer can be faster and still effective.
Speech
Noise (shifted to align)

3 Shape to ILTASS

Apply a spectral correction filter so the speech matches the International Long-Term Average Speech Spectrum (Byrne et al., 1994). Then generate new noise from the shaped speech.

G. O'Beirne, University of Canterbury, 2026. ILTASS data: Byrne et al. (1994), full-resolution spectrum.
